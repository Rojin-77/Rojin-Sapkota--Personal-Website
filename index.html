The datasets were merged and cleaned via Power Query in Excel, then imported into Power BI for advanced visualization. Key activities included creating DAX measures for calculations, implementing data modeling for relationships, performing ETL processes for transformation, and designing user-friendly interactive elements to deliver actionable insights on turnover, trends, and performance indicators.
